## Design Context

### Users
Personal/family tool for restoring and organizing scanned old photos. Primary user is technical (developer), but family members may also use it. Not a public-facing product — a private utility.

### Brand Personality
Utilitarian, efficient, straightforward. Get out of the way and let people work with their photos.

### Aesthetic Direction
Keep it simple. Current shadcn-style look is the right foundation — polish rather than redesign. Function over form. Photos should be the visual focus, not the UI chrome.

### Design Principles
1. **Efficiency first** — minimize clicks, maximize throughput for batch workflows
2. **Photos are the content** — UI should recede, photos should dominate
3. **Accessible to family** — clear labels, obvious actions, no jargon
4. **Simple over clever** — standard patterns over novel interactions
